Ottawa 67's

Save Big Campaign

The Ottawa 67’s campaign was a campaign designed to get consumers to buy tickets in large quantities. The focus was to target groups and teams by offering discounted ticket pricing for bulk purchasing. Pieces created for the campaign were a bus transit sign, two print advertisement adds, two web banners, and a facebook timeline.

Concepts

The creative process began with research that was used to develop three seperate campaign concepts. The concepts created were Save Big, Breakaway Deals, and We are the Future. Once the Concepts were established, each was sketched out on minimized banner sizes to show the client.

Photography

Photography was a key source in creating the various forms of advertisement. All photos were personally taken, manipulated and enhanced through the use of photoshop.

Final Product

The chosen concept for the Ottawa 67’s ticket campaign was Save Big. The concept focuses on the key player on any hockey team, the goaltender. A goalie is the last line of defence whose sole goal is to save pucks. This can be seen as a direct comparison to the consumer, whose main goal is to save money. This made for the perfect slogan to focus on for advertising the campaign.
