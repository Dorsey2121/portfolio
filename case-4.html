In Bloom

In Bloom is a small, local, independent flower shop located in Kanata, Ontario. For this client I created a brand new web page that had online sales sore by 900% within the first month!

Objective

My business objective was to create a user-friendly website that would help my client grow her customer base. My plan was to create a sleek web design that followed her brand-guidlines as well as create a new icon logo for the business. Furthermore the website had to be easy to navigate for an older audience as well as support an online store.

CMS Implementation

To allow customizability of the website for the client, I implemeted Wordpress, a content management systems that builds the website. Wordpress allows me as the developer to choose what areas the client will be able to change and update. This allows me to ensure the client can keep their content up to date, without having to worry about breaking their website.
