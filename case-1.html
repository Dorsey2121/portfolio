X Factor

X-Factor is a golfing app designed to give users an added edge over the competition. The app is designed to replace the paper scorecard, with digital scoring and previewed hole overviews. Users will now be able to enter live scores, track past statistics and store multiple scorecards all on their phone!

User Interface

The X-factor interface was designed to enhance and replace the paper scorecard. The app features hole overviews, green previews and added virtual putting assistance. The app also features scoring inputs, live scoreboards, and all-time statistics to track past rounds.

Creative Process

The creative process consisted of many steps. The process began with market research and idea formation. Once a solid idea was constructed, initial sketches and icons were created. The final step were constructing the interface in Xd and animating it in Illustrator and After Effects.
