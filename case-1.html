Paul Dorsey

Graphic Designer

X Factor is a golfing app designed to give users an added edge over the competition. The app features previewed shot distances, green previews, and a virtual first person slope reader. The app also serves as a paperless stat tracker for round scores, leaderboards, and all-time statistics.

Visual Design

Typography

X Factor strictly follows Apples typography guidelines. Apples system typeface is San Francisco, which is a neo-grotesque sans-serif typeface. San Francisco is extremely legible at all sizes and is used by the app for a consistent and familiar experience.

Iconography
