X Factor

X Factor is a golfing app designed to give users an added edge over the competition. The app features previewed shot distances, green previews, and a virtual first person slope reader. The app also serves as a paperless stat tracker for round scores, leaderboards, and all-time statistics.

Creative Process

The creative process began with market research, mind mapping, and mood boards. Once a solid foundation of ideas were created, prototyping through sketches were created.

Icons

The X-Factor icons were created to guide the user through navigation. The icons were designed to be aesthetically pleasing, as well as being easily recognizable.

User Experience

The X Factor app was designed in Adobe XD. The app allows users to pan through the different holes, view shot distances, shot previews, a mobile scorecard, and much more!
