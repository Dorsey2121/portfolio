About Me

My biggest passions in life are sports and design. My dad was a professional football player and my mom is a florist, so it has always been in my blood. Like many kids growing up in Canada, my first love was hockey. I played as a goalie in some of the highest leagues in the world. Although making the big save in front of thousands of people was exciting on its own, it was the in game entertainment and advertisement that excited me even more. When hockey came to an end there was little doubt about where my heart would lead me next, I would become a graphic designer.
