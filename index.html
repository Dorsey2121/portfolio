About Me

My name is Paul Dorsey and I am an experienced graphic designer within the Ottawa area. With a diploma in Graphic Design, a background in customer service, and the skills needed to create exactly what my clients are looking for, I am always open to meeting new clients and seeing what I can contribute to their project. I am open to opportunities to contribute my expertise knowledge in creating design solutions with the use of web design, motion graphics, layout design, and branding. I look forward to discussing potential positions at your business or organization!
